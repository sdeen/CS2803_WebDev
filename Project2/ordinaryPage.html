About

Thief is a stealth action video game. The first one was released in 1998. In 2014 the expanded version of Thief was out. In this game the player takes the role of Garrett, a master thief. The objective of this game is to sneak and avoid fighting with enemies and steal valuable items. The player has to play the game very carefully because if any noise is made, enemies will become alert. In each mission players have to explore more maps and find secret paths. One of the best aspects of the game is that it has many different endings.
